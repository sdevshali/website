Services


Transformation to NFV in 8 Logical Steps (V8LS), to help organisations transform into an efficient way of working with NFV. From procurement all the way to Life Cycle Management.

VoerEir has developed the V8LS methodology and provides transformational consulting based on this methodology. The cornerstone in the methodology is a clear definition of the demarcation point between the VNF applications and the NFVI cloud infrastructure. A clear demarcation point is key for successful NFV transformation and impact the way VNF applications and NFVI cloud infrastructure are designed, procured, deployed, tested, validated and operated. VoerEir has a unique industry understanding how the technical considerations impact the overall transformation.
