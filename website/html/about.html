About Voereir


We are a start-up founded mid 2015 and our focus is to help operators succeed with their NFV transformation. We have a list of customers, we are really proud of; including several Tier 1s, as customers to both consulting engagements and the Touchstone product, launched in Q3 2016.

Our team has many years of experience from OpenStack, NFV, Telecom application development and VNF onboarding. We continue to upgrade our knowledge base through industry cooperation, open source community engagement, cooperation with universities and, most important, assignments to evaluate and help to improve many different NFV infrastructures.

Our focus is to continue to develop an industry leading offering consisting of test suites, processes and expertise that will help cloud transformation projects reduce cost, risk and time to successful implementation.

VoerEir address a global market, with offices in Delhi and Stockholm
